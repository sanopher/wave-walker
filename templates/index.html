The city has fallen under the shadow of a terrifying monster called the Shadow Maw. Its darkness has swallowed the streets, silenced the people, and locked the land in fear.

Only one hero can stand against it: Wave Walker. To defeat the beast, he must recover the three fragments of the ancient sword, each hidden inside a deadly maze beneath the city.

Once the pieces are gathered, Wave Walker will forge the sword once more and free the city from the monster’s grip.
